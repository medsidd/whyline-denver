WhyLine Denver - Denver Transit Analytics

Free analytics for Denver RTD bus service. Ask questions about transit reliability, delays, equity gaps, and service quality in plain English.

What You Can Analyze:

  • 🚌 Bus Reliability - Which routes are most reliable? When do delays happen?
  • 📊 Equity Gaps - Where do vulnerable communities face poor transit service?
  • ⚠️ Safety - Which bus stops are near high-crash areas?
  • 🌦️ Weather Impact - How does snow and rain affect bus performance?
  • 📈 Real-time Data - Access live RTD GTFS data and historical trends

Launch the WhyLine Denver App

Data sources: Denver RTD GTFS, Denver Open Data, NOAA Weather, U.S. Census ACS
